Stress & Burnout

Stress is a natural response to challenging situations, but chronic stress can lead to burnout, especially in academic or work-related contexts.

Causes

Causes of stress and burnout include:

  • High academic or work pressure with little time for rest.
  • Financial concerns or social expectations.
  • Lack of work-life balance and overcommitment.

Signs

Signs of stress and burnout include:

  • Physical symptoms like headaches, fatigue, or insomnia.
  • Emotional exhaustion, irritability, or feeling overwhelmed.
  • Decreased performance and difficulty focusing.

Prevention

Managing stress and preventing burnout involves:

  • Prioritizing tasks and setting realistic goals.
  • Taking regular breaks and engaging in relaxation activities.
  • Seeking support from friends, family, or counselors.
